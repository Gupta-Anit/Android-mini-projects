Timeriffic does one thing: it can mute or un-mute the global Android ringer at specified time and days.

For example you tell Timeriffic to:

  • At 9 PM on Friday, mute the ringer and enable vibration.
  • At 9 AM on Monday, restore the ringer and revert the vibration to its default.

Version adds the following new features:

  • Profiles let you group actions together and enable or disable them at once.
  • Actions can now be set for given days, so for example you can have actions for the week days and others for the week-end.
A couple of default profiles are provided to get you started.

To edit a profile, use long press on a profile item or an action item. A menu will appear to let you edit, insert or delete the item.
